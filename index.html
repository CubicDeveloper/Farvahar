Our Story

About Farvahar Studio

Farvahar Studio is an Iranian creative studio dedicated to making high-quality video games, professional video edits, websites, and other digital creations. We draw inspiration from our rich Persian heritage to craft modern digital experiences with soul and excellence.

Founded on the belief that Iranian culture — from the grandeur of Persepolis to the wisdom of Zarathustra, from the poetry of Hafez to the fire of Ahura Mazda — holds within it stories that the world deserves to experience in the language of the 21st century: interactive media, cinematic storytelling, and immersive digital worlds.

Good Thoughts, Good Words, Good Deeds — in our craft, as in our lives.

— Zoroastrian teaching, the heart of Farvahar Studio

Every pixel, every frame, every line of code is authored with pride in our heritage and a deep respect for the craft. We are storytellers first — and the tools of Unreal Engine, After Effects, and modern web technologies are merely the brushes with which we paint our vision.

Whether you are an Iranian abroad longing for a digital connection to your roots, a gamer seeking a world you have never explored before, or a client who needs a digital presence built with taste and intelligence — Farvahar Studio is here to create something extraordinary with you.
